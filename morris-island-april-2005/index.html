Morris Island - Ice out April 2005



We decided to go for a spring stroll and see how the ice out was going. The water was way down and most of the ice was gone except from shorelines.



Usually the rocks we're climbing over are covered in water. You can canoe in the area and its pretty safe despite the dam being close by.



Geoff was having lots of rock climbing fun and surprisingly no wet shoes were created during our rock hopping episode.



You can almost see the dam in the distance from the shots below. The water was being kept low so the spring floods can fill the lake.




The ice was still about 18 inches thick. We found some around 3 ft thick right along the shorline.




Dad and G. Self Portrait



The last thing we saw before we left was a snapping turtle coming out of hiding He was in about 6 inches of water and was going back to sleep in the muck. By the time we passed by, he had buried himself and was gone.
